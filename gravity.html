Welcome to Gravitaty!

Use the cursor to place bodies on the canvas.

Drag the cursor to set an initial speed of the new body.

Press "start" to begin the interaction between placed bodies.

Use arrow keys to change the speed of animation.

Bodies can merge with respectively increased mass and radius.

Press Reset to clear the canvas and to start from scratch.

Enjoy.
